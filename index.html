Doberman
Dobermans are compactly built dogs, muscular, fast and athletic. They require daily exercise to help burn off heir large amounts of energy. Considered the fifth-smartest dog breed in the world, it is loyal in protecting its people and always alert.
Chocolate Labrador
Labradors are friendsly, outgoing, and high spirited companions. Labradors are good family dogs since they can get along well with small children because of their trinability and perffect size. They have plenty of energy so they are able to do as much acitivites as they can with their humans.
Samoyed
Samoyeds are smart, social and friendly dogs who require lots of love and attention. Samoyeds, the smiling sled dogs, were bred for hard work in the world's coldest locales. They make a good family dog because they are natural born cuddle bug who likes to snuggle with their humans more than anything.
Rottweiler
Well trained and socialized rottweilers get along nicely with other people. Although, males in particular can be a bit agressive and dominant. They are well known to be powerful, protective and famous for their instinct for guarding. One of the most popular dog breeds in the United States.
Border Collie
Border Collies were originally bred to herd sheeps. They're famous for using “the eye”— staring intensely at members of the flock to intimidate them. Highly smart because capability of learning a remarkable number of words and commands, and they are happiest when they are put to work every day.
